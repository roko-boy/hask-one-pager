Campaign Types
Type 1 — Trial Driver

Free Product / BOGO

Full cashback on purchase — one redemption per shopper. Ideal for driving first-time trial of the new packaging at Target. Best for reactivating legacy shoppers who haven't bought since the rebrand.

Type 2 — Repeat Purchase

$ Off Discount

Multi-redemption cashback offer. Converts first-time buyers into repeat shoppers. Auto-layers on top of Offer 1 as a retargeting follow-up — completely automated.

Type 3 — Loyalty

Punch Card Program

Structured repeat-purchase loop. Example: Buy 2 save $1.50 → Buy 3 save $2 → Buy 4 get BOGO. Builds the same community mechanic winning for indie brands — now at HASK scale.
