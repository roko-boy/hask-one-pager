Campaign Types
Offer Type 1 — Trial Driver

Free Product / BOGO Offer

Full cashback on purchase — one redemption per shopper. Best for driving first-time trial of the new packaging at Target. Ideal for reactivating legacy shoppers who haven't bought since the rebrand.

Offer Type 2 — Repeat Purchase

$ Off Discount

Multi-redemption cashback offer. Converts first-time buyers into repeat shoppers. Can layer automatically on top of Offer 1 as a retargeting follow-up — no manual work required.

Offer Type 3 — Loyalty

Punch Card Program

Structured repeat-purchase loop. Example: Buy 2 save $1.50 → Buy 3 save $2 → Buy 4 get BOGO. Creates the same community flywheel that indie brands use — now available to HASK.
